Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Skip2Times ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

Skip2Times stores all learning progress, quiz results, and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Practice session completions
  • Quiz scores and history
  • Unlocked multiplication tables
  • App preferences and settings

No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

Advertisements

Skip2Times may display advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and similar technologies to provide relevant advertising. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our app and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

In-App Purchases

Skip2Times offers an optional ad-free experience through in-app purchase. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play and Apple App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Skip2Times is designed to be safe for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app functions entirely offline for core learning features, and progress data remains on the device.

Data Security

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

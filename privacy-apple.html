Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Skip2Times app on the Apple App Store.

Introduction

Skip2Times ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

Skip2Times stores all learning progress, quiz results, and settings locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Practice session completions
  • Quiz scores and history
  • Unlocked multiplication tables
  • App preferences and settings

Parental Controls

If you enable a parental password, it is stored securely on your device using the iOS Keychain (via SecureStore). The password is never transmitted off your device.

No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as:

  • Names or email addresses
  • Precise location data
  • Usage analytics

Advertising

The iOS App Store version of Skip2Times does not display third-party advertising and does not make ad requests.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

Skip2Times uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases (for example, unlocking premium content and restoring purchases). RevenueCat may process anonymous identifiers and App Store receipt information to validate purchases and manage entitlements. Skip2Times does not send learning progress, quiz results, or app settings to RevenueCat.

For more information, please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Skip2Times offers an optional premium experience through in-app purchase to unlock all multiplication tables. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Skip2Times is designed to be safe for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app functions entirely offline for core learning features, and progress data remains on the device.

Data Security

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • Request information about data practices
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@skip2times.com
